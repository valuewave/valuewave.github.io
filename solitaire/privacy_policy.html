1. Introduction

ValueWave ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Cosmic Solitaire mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and shared when you use our App.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. Cosmic Solitaire is designed to be a simple, offline solitaire card game that does not collect or store any personal information from users.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information Collected Directly by Us

We do not collect, store, or process any personal information. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email address, phone number, etc.)
  • Location data
  • Photos, videos, or other media files
  • Contact information
  • Device information
  • Game play data or statistics
  • Any other personal or sensitive user data

The App functions entirely offline and does not require an internet connection to play the game. All game data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

Our App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. Google AdMob may collect certain information to provide personalized advertising. This information may include:

The collection and use of information by Google AdMob is governed by Google's Privacy Policy. For more information, please refer to:

  • Google Privacy Policy

3. How We Use Information

Since we do not collect any personal information, we do not use any personal data for any purpose. Google AdMob uses the information it collects to:

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share any personal information with third parties because we do not collect any personal information. Google AdMob may share data as described in their privacy policy.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Since we do not collect any personal information, we do not retain or store any user data. When you uninstall the App, all local game data stored on your device is automatically deleted.

For information collected by Google AdMob, please refer to Google's data retention policies.

6. Your Privacy Choices

6.1 Opt-Out of Personalized Advertising

You can opt-out of personalized advertising through your device settings:

For Android devices:

  • Go to Settings → Google → Ads
  • Enable "Opt out of Ads Personalization"

For iOS devices:

  • Go to Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking
  • Disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track"
  • Go to Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising
  • Disable "Personalized Ads"

6.2 Data Access and Deletion

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no user data for you to access or request deletion of. Simply uninstalling the App will remove all local game data from your device.
